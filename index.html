Seller Financing

Seller Financed Mortgages are the alternative to expensive/hard to obtain bank mortgage home purchase financing. While banks pay out diminimus interest on checking accounts and then use deposits to create high interest mortgage loans for their own benefit., buyers and sellers can both win when banks are bypassed via Seller Financed Mortgages arranged seller financing.

  • Learn more

Benefits

Many of the impediments to a home sale in the current environment can be addressed via seller financing:

  • High home prices/scarce supply-larger mortgages required
  • High mortgage rates-higher monthly payment requirements impede sales
  • Banks raise and adjust mortgage rates daily and have paperwork and underwriting standards that can be slow, difficult and frustrating - here, buyers and sellers control this process directly
  • Flexible pricing and structural options for buyers; attractive investment returns and possible capital gains deferral for sellers allows for optimized financing terms

How to grow the business?

Go to Market Strategy:

    Sales Tactics & Channels
  • Focus sales outreach on educating potential customers on faster, easier digital mortgage options as a solution to commonly voiced frustrations
  • Highlight ability to pre-approve buyers more efficiently to strengthen offers
  • Emphasize providing home valuations and potential equity taps to maximize seller profits
  • Offer dedicated loan officers specifically for real estate agents to facilitate coordination

    Customer Journey Mapping
  • Map an online-first, paperless process for higher convenience
  • Ensure a user-friendly digital application and portal to minimize complexity
  • Provide faster pre-approvals to support buyers with transaction timing needs
  • Design closing process to digitally capture equity for sellers rather than lump payout

    Marketing Tactics & Campaigns
  • Run social media ads showcasing customer testimonials on mortgage experience
  • Create equity harvesting ROI calculators for sellers as lead gen tools
  • Host webinars for real estate agents to provide program training and education
  • Promote first-time buyer eligibility and benefits available including low down payments
  • Sponsor local real estate and fintech innovation events as an innovative brand
    The key is positioning the mortgage company as the next-generation, digital-first solution to fundamentally improve and modernize the lending experience for all involved parties - buyers, sellers, and agents alike. The marketing, sales and supporter journeys should align to this overarching narrative and value proposition.
